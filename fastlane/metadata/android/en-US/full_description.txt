<p><i>Glucose Data Handler</i> receives glucose values from LibreLinkUp, <a href='https://github.com/j-kaltes/Juggluco' target='_blank' rel='nofollow noopener'>Juggluco</a> or <a href='https://github.com/NightscoutFoundation/xDrip' target='_blank' rel='nofollow noopener'>xDrip+</a> and provides widgets, notifications, sends glucodata broadcasts to other apps and more. The app also provides support for WearOS devices, Android Auto and Tasker.</p>
